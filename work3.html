About CDAC

A leading global company in the technology, e-commerce, and cloud computing industry was founded in 1994 and is headquartered in the United States, employing around 1.5 million people worldwide under the leadership of a well-known CEO. The company generates over $550 billion in annual revenue with a net income of approximately $30 billion and a market capitalization exceeding $1.5 trillion. Its core business segments include an extensive online marketplace offering products across multiple categories with significant contributions from third-party sellers, a highly dominant cloud computing division providing infrastructure and AI services, subscription-based services such as video streaming and premium delivery, and a robust logistics network ensuring efficient global distribution. The company is recognized for its strong customer-centric approach, continuous investment in artificial intelligence and automation, and rapid expansion, particularly during pandemic years, while also facing challenges such as regulatory scrutiny, high operational costs, and intense competition from other global players.
